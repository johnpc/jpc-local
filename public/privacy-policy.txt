PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: December 30, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Ann Arbor local information application (the "Service").

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users of this application.

DATA SOURCES

Our application displays publicly available information from third-party sources:
- Weather data from RSS feeds
- Real estate listings from public sources
- News articles from MLive.com
- Reddit posts from r/AnnArbor (public subreddit)
- Craigslist listings (public marketplace)
- Emergency alerts from public safety sources

All displayed content is publicly available information that we aggregate and present for your convenience.

LOCAL STORAGE

The application may use your device's local storage to:
- Cache content for improved performance
- Remember your tab preferences
- Store temporary data for offline functionality

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our application retrieves data from external RSS feeds and APIs. When you click links to view full articles or listings, you will be directed to third-party websites that have their own privacy policies:
- MLive.com
- Reddit.com
- Craigslist.org
- Real estate listing websites

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

COOKIES AND TRACKING

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect personal information.

DATA SECURITY

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data at risk. All displayed information is already publicly available.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Service does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, this application is safe for all ages.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the application's support channels.

This privacy policy is effective as of the date stated above and applies to all users of the application.
